F.A.Q.

Is it possible to analyze foreign profile in LinkedIn?

You can analyze any profile, but there should be as much skills as possible.

Why do I get message "check if the link is valid"?

Your link isn't from LinkedIn or not valid. However, you can leave field with LinkedIn empty and enter your skills in next field.

Why do I get a few spheres of work?

Your skills fit many spheres, so it is possible to choose what you like more.
